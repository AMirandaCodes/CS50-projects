My writing

Throughout my life, I’ve had many interests — from drawing to magic — but one passion has always remained constant: creative writing.

I’ve always enjoyed writing, especially fiction and short tales. My style focuses on short-form writing — unlike novels, my pieces are usually only a few paragraphs long.
