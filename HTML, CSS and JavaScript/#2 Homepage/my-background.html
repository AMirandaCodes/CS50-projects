My origins

I was born in Barcelona, Spain. However, neither of my parents are Spanish — my mom is from Colombia, and my dad is from Peru!

I grew up in Barcelona, and at the age of 15, I moved to the UK, where I’ve lived ever since.
I speak 3 languages: Spanish, English and Calatan! I completed my secondary education in the UK and went to university in London.

My technical side

I’ve always been good with numbers, which naturally led me to study engineering at university.

My creative side

Apart from my technical side, I’ve always had a strong creative drive. Most of my creative work has involved drawing and writing.
